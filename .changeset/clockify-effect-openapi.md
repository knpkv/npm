Replace the openapi-fetch Clockify surface with a Schema-validated client generated by Effect's official OpenAPI generator. The old raw client, `ClockifyApiError`, `toEffect`, and `FetchClientError` exports are removed; consumers now use the generated `ClockifyApi` operations or the authenticated service conveniences.
